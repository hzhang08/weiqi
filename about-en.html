About Us

Strategic mastery, shaping futures.

Legend Go Academy's new course is designed for children aged 6-12 years old in the East Bay Tri-Valley area.

We are enrolling two experimental small classes (Beginner Class) with 5 students each, until full capacity.

The current term is two months.

Coach Henry is an amateur 5-dan Go player, currently working as a software engineer at LinkedIn, and achieved provincial runner-up during his youth.

We welcome students with high interest. Feel free to inquire for details.
